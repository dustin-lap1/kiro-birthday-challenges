# Vibe2Cloud — One Pager

## The Problem

Teams prototype fast with AI app builders, then stall at the last mile: getting
that prototype onto real, production-grade cloud infrastructure without a
dedicated DevOps hire. The gap between "it works on my laptop" and "it is live,
secure, and scalable" is where most momentum dies.

## The Solution

Vibe2Cloud automatically migrates applications from vibe coding platforms to dedicated cloud service providers. 

## Why Now

| Trend | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| AI app builders exploding | More prototypes need a real backend |
| Serverless is default | Costs scale to zero, no servers to manage |
| Solo founders + small teams | Nobody wants to hire DevOps on day one |

## Traction

- Private beta with early design partners
- Built entirely on AWS serverless primitives
- Repeatable, Terraform-defined deployments
